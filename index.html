My Approach

My path to wellness coaching has been a unique blend of analytical rigor and creative exploration. I'm grounded in a deep understanding of the human mind, from an MA in Clinical Psychology to a PhD in Mathematics applied to computational neuroscience and decision-making. I’ve spent years studying how we think, both academically as a postdoc at UPenn and professionally as a data scientist.

But we are more than just our minds. I believe in a holistic path that embraces the whole self. This belief comes from my own life-long explorations: from the discipline of 10+ years of piano and kung-fu to the creative freedom of improv acting, clowning, and dance. My passion for yoga, tantra, and ancient wisdom traditions informs a practice rooted in both body and spirit.

Born in Mexico and raised in France, I'm a trilingual (French, Spanish, English) individual who has lived and studied across the UK and US. I love connecting with people from all walks of life. My curiosity is wide—from metal music to quantum physics, from philosophy to simply laughing.

I believe in human potential, and my core motivation is to help you find your own authentic path to happiness and well-being.
