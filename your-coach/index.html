ADRIAN RADILLO

FOUNDER & LEAD COACH

I offer online coaching for anyone seeking meaning, joy, fulfilling relationships, reduced anxiety, and professional satisfaction. I am a clinical psychologist trained in France (2009) and a former computational neuroscience researcher in the U.S. (2014–2020). Trilingual in French, English, and Spanish, I also practice yoga and kung-fu daily and draw on acting, music, and scientific training to enrich my therapeutic approach.

My method blends body awareness, tailored movement exercises, and artistic expression—tools that help you reconnect with yourself beyond words. From the first session, you define your success criteria, and together we work toward visible changes within three months.
